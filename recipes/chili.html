Chili

A picture of our scrumptious beef chili.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small diced onion
  • 3 cans of diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans of beans
  • 2 tbsp chili powder

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook ground beef on medium heat until browned while stirring frequently.
  2. Add onion to the pot and cook for 5 minutes until soft.
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients and stir to combine. Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce heat and simmer for 10 minutes.
