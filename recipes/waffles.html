Waffles

A picture of our luxurious waffles

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour, salt, sugar and baking powder in a mixing bowl.
  2. In another bowl, mix the milk and egg together.
  3. Add flour mixture and stir until just mixed.
  4. Cook on pan with oil to prevent sticking and brown on both sides.
