Cookies

A photo of our delicious cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1.75 cups flour
  • 1 large egg
  • 0.5 cup butter
  • 0.5 cup sugar
  • 0.5 teaspoon baking soda
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chocolate chips
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Melt butter and combine with sugar in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Stir in vanilla extract and egg to incorporate into the mixture.
  4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt to the bowl and mix until dough is soft and slightly sticky.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop out roughly 1.5 tablespoons of dough and place balls with 2 inch margins onto baking sheet.
  7. Bake for 8 minutes or until golden brown.
